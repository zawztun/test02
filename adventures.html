Winter Adventure

Snowshoeing

Weave in and out of spruce-fir forest along a babbling creek and learn about the animals that inhabit the Rocky Mountains in winter. Naturalists from the Aspen Center for Environmental Studies lead twice-daily guided tours around both Snowmass and Aspen Mountain. You’ll also find a wide range of maintained trails in and around the town.

Summer Adventure

Rafting & Kayaking

For the ultimate Colorado experience, whitewater rafting and kayaking are adventures that keep you on your toes and always ready for more. We love the guides over at Blazing Adventures, who know every inch of Aspen’s waters. From the mellowest of streams to its world-class rapids.

Skiing Adventure

Skiing

SKIING Four mountains on one ticket. Pretty incredible, right? Throw in unique mixes of seductive terrain and it’s pretty hard to wrap your head around. Don’t worry, we’re here to help. Whether looking for long, rolling cruisers for an all-in-the-family affair, or steep cliffs to overcome your fear of heights, we’ll point you in the right direction.
